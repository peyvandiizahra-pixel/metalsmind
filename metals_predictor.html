Architecture

How the model works

A deep learning ensemble combining Convolutional and Recurrent neural networks, trained on 23 technical indicators across 25 years of market data.

STEP 01 · DATA
25 years of market data
6,400+ trading days per metal from Yahoo Finance. Covers multiple market cycles, crises, bull and bear markets from 2000 to 2026.
STEP 02 · FEATURES
23 technical indicators
RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, EMA crossovers, volume signals, price momentum across 1, 3, 7, 14 and 30 day windows.
STEP 03 · CNN
Pattern detection
Convolutional layers scan 3-day windows across 60 days of data. 128 filters identify local price patterns like RSI divergences and volume spikes.
STEP 04 · LSTM
Sequence memory
Long Short-Term Memory layers read CNN patterns in sequence. Connects short-term signals to long-term behaviour learned from 25 years of history.
STEP 05 · INTERVALS
4 dedicated models
Separate CNN+LSTM model trained for each time horizon. 1-week model optimised for weekly patterns, 6-month model for macro trends.
STEP 06 · OUTPUT
Price + confidence range
Model outputs predicted return percentage, converted to price. Confidence range shows low and high estimates for risk assessment purposes.
